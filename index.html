How It Works

1

Let's Get to Know Each Other

We'll start with a friendly video chat where we can talk about your style interests, what you're looking for, and any questions you might have. This helps me understand your preferences and comfort level.

2

Plan Your Shopping Adventure

Once we've connected, we'll schedule a time that works for both of us to go shopping together. I'll make sure we have plenty of time to explore and try things on without feeling rushed.

3

I'll Do the Research

Based on our conversation, I'll research stores and locations that match your style preferences and comfort level. I'll send you a list of places to review, and we can discuss which ones feel right for you.

4

Time to Shop!

We'll meet up and have a fun, supportive shopping experience together. I'll be there to help you find pieces that make you feel confident and authentic, offering encouragement every step of the way.
